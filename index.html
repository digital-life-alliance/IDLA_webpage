Mission

The International Digital Life Alliance (IDLA) brings together leading scientists, technologists, and institutions to chart the next frontier in biology: digital simulation of life. Our mission is to enable open, interoperable, and Al-enhanced approaches to understanding the complexity of life-from molecules to systems. We coordinate research, data exchange, and infrastructure development to foster a new ecosystem of computational biology and digital biomedicine that serves humanity.

GOALS

Digital
Simulation
of Life

We aim to build digital representations of human biological systems, from molecules and cells to complex tissues-enabling virtual experiments, predictive modeling, and deeper insight into biology.

Open
Biological
Data
Infrastructure

We promote international access to high-quality, interoperable biological data-ensuring that knowledge is discoverable, reusable, and secure.

Large
Biological
Model
Sharing

We support the development, refinement, and global sharing of large-scale biological models, fostering community-driven innovation and reproducibility in computational biology.

Al-Driven
Discovery

We advance life science and medical research by leveraging next-generation Al tools to analyze, model, and predict biological phenomena—accelerating insights into health, disease, and therapy

Founding Institution

Guangzhou Laboratory, established in 2021 in Guangzhou, China, is a pioneering research institution dedicated to advancing respiratory diseases and their prevention. The lab cultivates a dynamic, multidisciplinary team to unravel the pathogenesis of respiratory infections and chronic conditions, while drive breakthroughs in therapeutic development, vaccine innovation, and prevention technologies. Through integrated research platforms, Guangzhou Lab accelerates the translation of scientific discoveries into transformative solutions.

With a mission rooted in scientific rigor, global impact, and technological foresight, Guangzhou Laboratory serves as a founding institution of the International Digital Life Alliance, working to shape a healthier and more resilient future through digital life sciences.
